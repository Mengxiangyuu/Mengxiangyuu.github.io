SPSS and questionnaire analysis

7 steps to analysis questionnaire

Step 1 Descriptive Statistics Analysis

These analysis is used to show that the random of sample data.

分析→描述统计→频率

Step 2 Reliability analysis

Analysis reliability of data

分析→度量→可靠性分析

if Cronbach’s alpha > 0.7;

The data is reliable

step 3 Status analysis

Calculate dimension score(average score)

转化→计算变量

step 4 Differentiation analysis

Find specific people and analyze them. eg: Determine whether gender has an impact on the experience

2 methods

  1. Student’s t test

2 variables eg: (male and female)

分析→比较平均值→独立样本t检验

  1. ANOVA

more than 2 variables

if Levin’s variance > 0.05 ;

no impact on gender and experience;

分析→比较平均值→单因素方差分析

step 5 Correlation analysis

分析→相关→双变量

bigger than 0 → positive correlation

pearson < 0.05 correlation

step 6 Affect the factor analysis

分析→回归→线性

if R**2 > 30%:

These variables will influence result

step 7 conclusion

data analysis conclusion != conclusion
